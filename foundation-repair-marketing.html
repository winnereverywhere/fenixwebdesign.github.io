Foundation repair businesses typically see increased inquiries within 2-3 weeks since homeowners often research thoroughly before contacting contractors. Quality leads usually increase significantly within the first month as people actively seek professional solutions.

Yes, we adjust your marketing throughout the year, emphasizing different foundation concerns during different seasons - settlement issues after dry periods, water damage prevention before rainy seasons, and general assessments during moderate weather periods.
