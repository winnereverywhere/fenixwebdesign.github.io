Pay Per Click Advertising Questions

Get answers to common questions about PPC advertising and how it can help your business attract more qualified customers and grow your revenue.

How quickly will I see results from PPC advertising?

PPC campaigns typically start generating leads within 24-48 hours of launch. However, optimal performance usually develops over 2-4 weeks as we gather data and optimize your campaigns. Unlike SEO which takes months, PPC provides immediate visibility and can start delivering qualified customers right away.

How much should I budget for PPC advertising?

PPC budgets depend on your market, competition, and growth goals. We typically recommend starting with a budget that allows for meaningful testing and optimization. Most successful campaigns see positive ROI within the first month, and we'll help you scale profitable campaigns as results improve.

What makes your PPC management different?

We focus on delivering qualified leads, not just clicks. Our campaigns target high-intent keywords and optimize for actual business results - phone calls, form submissions, and customers. We provide transparent reporting and continuously optimize to maximize your return on advertising spend.

Can you help if my current PPC campaigns aren't working?

Absolutely. We'll audit your existing campaigns to identify what's not working and why. Common issues include poor keyword selection, low-converting landing pages, and inadequate campaign optimization. We can typically improve underperforming campaigns significantly within the first month.

How do you ensure my ads reach the right customers?

We use advanced targeting including geographic location, demographics, device types, and search behavior patterns. We also use negative keywords to exclude irrelevant searches and continuously refine targeting based on which audiences convert best for your specific business.

What kind of reporting will I receive?

You'll receive detailed monthly reports showing leads generated, cost per lead, conversion rates, and return on ad spend. We also provide real-time dashboard access so you can monitor campaign performance anytime. Our reports focus on business metrics that matter, not just advertising statistics.
